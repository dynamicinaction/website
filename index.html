--- layout: archive author_profile: false excerpt: "Opening pathways for discovery, research, and innovation in health (and healthcare)." header: overlay_color: "#1E90FF" --- This project is a collaboration between patients and traditional researchers to explore the processes around discovery, research, and innovation in health and healthcare. The project is unique because it is led by a patient as Principal Investigator. The project is supported with grant funding by the Robert Wood Johnson Foundation.
